Hundreds of top online news publishers use Parse.ly to track content performance on their site. This report aggregates nearly 5 billion pageviews per month from over 300 million unique visitors to determine what the top sources of traffic are for major news publishers.

Below, we define "parse.ly sites" as traffic from one Parse.ly site to another. For example, traffic from Mashable to Arstechnica (both Parse.ly customers) will be listed in this category.

We define "news sites" as any other news sites outside of the Parse.ly network.

We define "google sites" as an aggregate of all Google-owned properties, e.g. Google.com, Google.ca, and Google News all fall under this.
